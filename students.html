Students

My main research interests are centered around future projections of sea-level rise and their impacts on coastal defense and adaptation decision-making. But, I also work on a diverse set of other topics. Check out the list of previous/ongoing student projects below or send me an email if you'd like to chat.

I am currently looking for students at all levels, and aim to create a research group with a diversity of culture, experiences and ways of thinking. If you are interested in chatting about research, potential projects or anything, feel free to shoot me an email or stop by my office.
